Sample Insurance Policies (INR) — Auto / Health / Life
------------------------------------------------------
This bundle contains synthetic policy PDFs for ML testing.
Each PDF includes: coverage, exclusions, premiums (₹), sum insured, deductibles, waiting periods,
claim settlement ratio, additional benefits, flexibility options, support contacts, validity dates,
and authorized signatory.

Files:
- Directory: out_policies/ (PDFs)
- CSV: policy_summary.csv
- README: README.txt
